Established in the year, 1988 Deep Hospital in Ludhiana has made a rather exceptional name in the city. According to many, the main doctor and founder director of Deep Hospital, Dr Baldeep Singh, classifies as one of the best pediatrician in Ludhiana. The stronghold, Deep Hospital has in the medical field, not only drawn in patients from in and around the vicinity but from across the region as well. The hospital is located in Model Town and can be found easily, conveyance is easily available and the place is well connected with all major road.

Deep Hospital is a 200 bedded multispecialty hospital providing affordable and comprehensive health - care services to patients under one roof. Deep Hospital's strength comes from it's eminent panel of medical professionals, many of whom are leaders in their respective fields along with the latest state of art equipment.
